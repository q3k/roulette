russianroulette.sh

what?

It's a russian roulette, but for your *nix machine. Fire away, and have fun things happen to your machine.

why?

curl -k $STUFF | sudo sh -

Have you ever though about how stupid this sort of installation mechanism is? All these hip new software libraries do this - 'to install me, just execute this ridiculously insecure thing on your shell! oh, and if that doesn't work, try with --insecure and sudo!'. I mean, seriously guys. Seriously. Don't do this.

A Hacker News article and comments section already describe it better than I could - and the comment thread also sparked the idea of a service such as this one.
